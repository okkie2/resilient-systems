# Resilient Systems

This site examines digital resilience as a practical problem of control.

It looks at what happens when infrastructure remains functional, but the assumptions behind it shift: law, ownership, platform policy, concentration, and exit cost.

The goal is not maximal independence. It is clearer judgment about where dependence becomes fragility, and what can still be changed before that fragility hardens.
