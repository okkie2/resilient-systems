This site documents a practical exploration of digital dependency and resilience.

The focus is not on tools themselves, but on the structural forces that shape them: jurisdictional exposure, ownership, exit costs, and service continuity.

Rather than offering product reviews or optimisation advice, the analysis draws on concrete migrations, failure cases, and real-world trade-offs, informed by systems thinking, resilience engineering, and platform governance.

The underlying question is simple:

> Who ultimately controls the systems we rely on — and what happens when those assumptions change?



Written by J. Okkinga


J. Okkinga is an established IT consultant with two decades of experience working on regulated digital systems, platform dependency, and operational resilience.
